Benefits of a virtual directory:

1. Keep track of numbers to record
2. Prioritize contacts.
3. Keep them up to date.
3. Stay organized with calls.

Keeping up with calls

Keeping up with calls enables us to maintain communication and connection with those most important to us, as well as professional networks. A contact app is a perfect way to maintain a balance between personal and professional calling needs.

Motivation

Ever forget your best friend's number? Or feel tired of the influx of calls you have to keep up with on the daily? What about all the times you've wanted to call that special person, but forgot to store their number in your phone? Never fear! Contact App is here! With contact app you'll never have to worry about losing people's numbers or asking mutual contacts for them or looking them up online. This application lets you record that list of numbers you never want to forget, and refer back to them when need be!

How to use Contact App

A step-by-step process!

1. Click the plus symbol to create a new entry.

2. Enter the first name, last name, and
additional contact information in the boxes
provided at the end of the contact list
3. Click Enter, then watch as the contact appears in the contact list!
